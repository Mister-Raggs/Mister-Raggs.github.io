## Overview

JobHunter is a comprehensive tool designed to automate the job search process, saving hours of manual effort while improving the quality of opportunities discovered.

## Features

- **Web Scraping**: Automatically collect job postings from multiple sources
- **Intelligent Filtering**: ML-based filtering to match your skills and preferences
- **Ranking Algorithm**: Rank opportunities by relevance and fit
- **Analysis Dashboard**: Visualize trends and insights from your job search
- **Export Options**: Generate reports in multiple formats

## Technologies

- Python (Pandas, BeautifulSoup, Scikit-learn)
- Flask/FastAPI
- PostgreSQL
- Selenium
