## Overview

JobHunter automates the job search process, saving hours of manual effort while improving the quality of opportunities discovered.

## Features

- **Web Scraping**: Automatically collect job postings from multiple sources
- **Intelligent Filtering**: ML-based filtering to match skills and preferences
- **Ranking Algorithm**: Rank opportunities by relevance and fit
- **Analysis Dashboard**: Visualize trends and insights from your job search
- **Export Options**: Generate reports in multiple formats

## Technologies

Python · Pandas · BeautifulSoup · Scikit-learn · FastAPI · PostgreSQL · Selenium
